MAONO AU-PM461TR USB Microphone Condenser Review

If you are looking for an affordable yet decent performing microphone for your podcast or streaming needs, then you are in luck. Today, we are taking a closer look at the MAONO AU-PM461TR USB Microphone Condenser. The MAONO AU-PM461TR USB Microphone Condenser is by far one of the most claimed budget king microphone on the market. Most people claim that it easily out best the “Microphone ng Bayan”, which is the BM-800, by a huge margin. Fortunately, we got on our hands the MAONO AU-PM461TR. So without further ado, let us take a closer look. Below is our unboxing video and microphone test. The MAONO AU-PM461TR boast a built-in professional sound chipset and 14mm electret condenser transducer. It also equipped with a 192kHz 24bit sampling rate, which lets the user capture a clear and smooth sound regardless if you are in a zoom meeting or video game streaming. The mic uses a USB A to USB B connection and its a plug and play. So you do not need any sort of external power or driver to use it. One notable feature of the microphone is the front knob dial that functions as a gain limiter. Increasing the gain value simply increases the loudness that the mic can record. However, there seems to be some sort of quality degradation when setting the gain value to its highest. Conclusion The Maono AU-PM461TR USB Gaming Microphone Condenser delivers what most have claimed it to be. For US$30, this is by far one, if not the best, of the best USB microphone condenser that you can buy on the market. Fortunately, there are some listings online that sell it cheaper than the retail price which is really a steal deal if you find one. Overall, if you have a budget and want a USB microphone that gives more than what you paid for, then we highly recommend checking out the Maono AU-PM461TR. Otherwise, if you have a couple more greens to squeeze out, then you might want to look for higher-end models.
